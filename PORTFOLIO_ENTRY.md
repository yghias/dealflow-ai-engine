# Portfolio Entry

## Project
dealflow-ai-engine

## Summary
An AI-driven deal intelligence and CRM lifecycle automation platform that detects external signals, identifies counterparties, generates evidence-backed strategies, dispatches tasks, and continuously improves through observed outcomes and analyst feedback.

## Why It Matters
The project demonstrates how modern data platforms can embed LLMs into operational workflows without sacrificing governance, reproducibility, or observability.

## Highlights
- Canonical data architecture across raw, staging, core, and mart layers.
- Modular Python services for ingestion, scoring, strategy generation, and CRM synchronization.
- Structured AI outputs with prompt versioning, evaluation, and review gates.
- Clear portfolio alignment for Senior Data Engineer, AI Engineer, and Principal Data Architect roles.
